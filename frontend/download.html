Download Assistance

  1. Visit the Downloads Page: Open the SafeSight website on your computer and navigate to the "Downloads" section.
  2. Select Your Region: Choose your country from the available options on the page to view AI models specific to your region.
  3. Connect Your SafeSight Device: Power on your SafeSight device and connect it to your computer using a USB cable.
  4. Choose the Model: Select the desired model and click on the "Flash Model" button.
  5. Website Device Detection: Allow the website to detect your device and grant any necessary permissions in your browser.
  6. Start the Flashing Process: Confirm the selected model and click on "Start Flashing." The website will transfer the model to your device.
  7. Wait for Completion: A progress bar will display during the flashing process. Once completed, a success message will appear.
  8. Verify the Installation: Disconnect the device, power it on, and test it to ensure the newly flashed model is functioning correctly.
