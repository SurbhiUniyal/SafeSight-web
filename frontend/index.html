Welcome to Safesight

SafeSight is a compact device designed to assist visually impaired individuals in identifying currency notes quickly and accurately. Equipped with AI-powered recognition, haptic feedback, and audio cues, it provides a reliable, easy-to-use solution for safe and independent transactions.

Press D to navigate to the Downloads page.

AI-Driven

The device leverages powerful AI algorithms to accurately identify different denominations. This intelligent system ensures quick and reliable recognition of currency notes.

Offline Functionality

The device works entirely offline, providing users with independence from internet connectivity. This feature is crucial for on-the-go use without relying on data services.

Dual Feedback

Users receive both audio and vibration feedback for every recognized denomination. This multi-sensory feedback ensures information is accessible through sight and touch.

Real-Time Processing

The device processes currency recognition in real-time, offering immediate responses as soon as a note is scanned. This ensures seamless and efficient usage in everyday situations.

Compact and Portable Design

Its lightweight, portable design makes it easy to carry anywhere. The device is small enough to fit in your pocket, enabling convenient and quick access to currency identification on the go.

Model Updates via USB

Users can update the AI models via a USB connection. This ensures the device stays up-to-date with new currency notes or changes, keeping it reliable and functional in the long term.

How to Use SafeSight

Insert the currency note into the designated slot and press the button to begin recognition. The device will announce the denomination audibly. If misaligned, it will prompt you to reinsert the note for accurate identification.
